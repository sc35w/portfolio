Projects

Project Highlights

  • Autonomous Ground Rover Platform
    Led the end-to-end development of an autonomous ground rover for industrial and exploration applications. Defined product strategy, managed cross-functional teams, and delivered a rugged, modular, and intelligent rover system under tight timelines.
  • AI-Enabled Robotic Arm for Fruit Plucking
    Developed an AI-powered robotic arm capable of identifying and plucking fruits such as strawberries and tomatoes. Trained a YOLO-based model for fruit detection, integrated with ROS2 and stepper motors for precise actuation, and engineered a digital joystick for both AI-enabled and manual control modes.
  • Moving Multi-Target System (MMTS)
    Designed and implemented a moving multi-target system for military and police firearms training. Utilized computer vision to automatically score shots and measure distance from the target center. Developed a feedback app to provide real-time alignment and performance insights to trainees, enhancing their marksmanship skills.
  • High-Performance Hot Surface Igniters
    Designed and developed high-performance hot surface igniters, utilizing advanced simulation (Hypermesh, MATLAB, ANSYS) and mathematical modeling to achieve 95% predictive accuracy. Increased manufacturing yield from 45% to 90% through process optimization and design improvements. Developed three patentable igniter designs focused on performance and durability.
  • Gas Valves and Ceramic Heaters
    Led the design and simulation of gas valves and ceramic heaters, optimizing for cost, safety, and compliance with international standards. Conducted CFD and structural analysis to enhance product reliability and efficiency.
  • Fabrication of Silicon Carbide-based Ceramic Liners
    Used Hypermesh and Nastran solvers to conduct comprehensive structural and thermal analysis of SiC liners for induction-based forging machines. Implemented design enhancements and advanced manufacturing techniques for improved durability and performance.
  • Water-Based Cooling Systems (30-150 kW)
    Designed and optimized water-based cooling systems for industrial applications, integrating pumps, heat exchangers, and coolant reservoirs for maximum efficiency and cost-effectiveness.
  • Material Extrusion-based Additive Manufacturing Machine
    Designed and fabricated a custom additive manufacturing machine for advanced ceramics, including hardware and software integration, and optimization of ceramic paste formulation using AI and machine learning.
  • Ride Vehicle Chassis Frame Analysis
    Conducted strength, fatigue, and bolt analysis for ride vehicle chassis used in amusement parks, utilizing HyperMesh and ANSYS. Developed Excel VBA tools for large-scale bolt analysis and collaborated with robotics experts for safety validation.
  • Robotic Dinosaur Body Frames (Multi-body Dynamics Simulation)
    Performed multi-body dynamics simulation and optimization for robotic dinosaur attractions, ensuring reliability and performance for theme park applications.
  • Finite Element Modeling and Simulation of Chassis Components
    Led FEA and simulation projects for automotive chassis components, achieving cost and weight reductions, and developing comprehensive CAE databases for major automotive parts.
